Senior Nurse - ICU

City General Hospital
New York, NY $75,000 - $95,000 Full-time
Featured

We are seeking a highly skilled and compassionate Senior Nurse to join our Intensive Care Unit (ICU) team. The ideal candidate will have extensive experience in critical care nursing and a strong commitment to patient well-being.

Job Description

As a Senior Nurse in the ICU, you will be responsible for providing direct patient care, managing complex medical cases, and collaborating with a multidisciplinary team to ensure optimal patient outcomes. You will also mentor junior staff and contribute to the continuous improvement of nursing practices.

Responsibilities

  • Provide comprehensive nursing care to critically ill patients.
  • Monitor patient vital signs, administer medications, and perform medical procedures.
  • Collaborate with physicians and other healthcare professionals to develop and implement patient care plans.
  • Educate patients and their families on health conditions and treatment plans.
  • Maintain accurate and detailed patient records.
  • Participate in unit meetings, quality improvement initiatives, and continuing education programs.
  • Supervise and guide junior nursing staff.

Requirements

  • Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) required; Master's preferred.
  • Current RN license in New York State.
  • Minimum of 3 years of experience in an ICU setting.
  • BLS and ACLS certification required.
  • Strong critical thinking, communication, and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced and high-pressure environment.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package.
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance.
  • 401(k) retirement plan with company match.
  • Paid time off and holidays.
  • Continuing education opportunities and tuition reimbursement.
  • Professional development and career advancement programs.
